Barça’s Trophies

FC Barcelona is one of the most decorated football clubs in the world. Over more than a century, the club has amassed an impressive array of domestic and international trophies.

The club has dominated La Liga for decades and is consistently among the top contenders in the UEFA Champions League. Barcelona has also enjoyed success in domestic cup competitions like the Copa del Rey and the Supercopa de España.

The treble-winning season in 2008–09 under Pep Guardiola remains one of the most iconic achievements in football history, with Barça winning La Liga, the Copa del Rey, and the UEFA Champions League in the same season.
